<p>I am thrilled to join this vibrant school community and share the educational
philosophy that will guide me in this journey. At the heart of my educational
philosophy lies a fundamental belief: every individual, whether staff member
or student, possesses unique potential and the capacity to achieve excellence.
This conviction drives my commitment to cultivating an enriching educational
environment where every individual strives to become the best version of
themselves.</p>
<p>Our approach to education is holistic, developing both the attitude and
aptitude of our students whilst simultaneously supporting our staff's professional
growth. As our school vision emphasises, the experiences we create should
enable students to work hard through diverse academic and non-academic
pursuits, whilst also allowing them to play hard, embracing the joy of
learning through a rich array of co-curricular activities. These experiences
not only enrich their educational journey but also build the future-ready
competencies and strong character they will need as responsible individuals.</p>
<p>This educational mission cannot be accomplished in isolation. I believe
deeply in the power of collaboration, working hand in hand with our dedicated
staff, supportive parents, school advisory committee, and broader community
stakeholders. When we unite our efforts and expertise, we create a robust
support network that empowers our students to discover and excel in their
areas of interest and strength.</p>
<p>Ultimately, our collective goal is to prepare students who are not only
academically capable but also confident, well-rounded individuals ready
to make a mark on the world as they transition to the next phase of their
lives. Through this collaborative and holistic approach, we can ensure
that every student leaves our school equipped with the skills, values,
and resilience they need to thrive in an ever-changing world.</p>
<p><strong>Ms Geralin Tan</strong>
<br><strong>Vice-Principal (Academic)</strong>
