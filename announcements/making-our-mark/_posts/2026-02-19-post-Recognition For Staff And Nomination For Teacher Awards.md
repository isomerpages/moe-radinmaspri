<p><strong><u>Recognition For Staff And Nomination For Teacher Awards</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Our staff work tirelessly for the holistic development of your child/ward.
We would like to encourage parents/guardians and our students to give words
of encouragement and affirmation to our staff periodically where due. You
can email our staff directly and/or send to our generic email address at
<a href="mailto:rmps@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">rmps@moe.edu.sg</a>.</p>
<p><strong><u>For English Teachers</u></strong>
</p>
<p>INSPIRING TEACHER OF ENGLISH AWARD 2026 is a national award for English
teachers in Singapore schools. It acknowledges teachers who ignite a love
for the English language and are effective in helping their students speak
and write accurately. We would like to invite you to nominate your child’s/ward’s
English teacher, you can download and complete the nomination form (
<a href="https://www.languagecouncils.sg/goodenglish/inspiring-teacher-of-english-award/nomination-information" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">NOMINATE YOUR ENGLISH LANGUAGE TEACHER TODAY!</a>) and submit to <a href="mailto:rmps@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">rmps@moe.edu.sg</a> by <strong>23 March 2026 (Monday).</strong>
</p>

<p><strong><u>For Mother Tongue Language (MTL) Teachers</u></strong>
</p>
<p>We would also like to invite you to consider nominating your child’s/ward’s <strong><u>Mother Tongue Language (MTL) Teachers</u> </strong>who
have made significant contributions to the teaching and learning of MTL
in our school. The awards also recognise the exemplary efforts of MTL teachers
who have successfully fostered in their students an appreciation for their
MTL and culture. To affirm their contributions to the teaching of MTL,
you can nominate our MTL teachers for their respective awards:</p>
