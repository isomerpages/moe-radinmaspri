<p>As part of our 100th Anniversary, we have launched 100 Acts of Sustainability,
a year-long initiative to educate our students that small daily actions
matter.&nbsp; Through simple acts like reducing waste, recycling and caring
for our green spaces, we hope to nurture lifelong eco-habits and grow eco-stewardship.&nbsp;</p>
