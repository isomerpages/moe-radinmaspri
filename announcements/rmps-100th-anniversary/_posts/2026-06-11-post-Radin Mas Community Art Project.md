<p>In celebration of our school’s 100<sup>th</sup> Anniversary, we have embarked
on an exciting Community Art Project in collaboration with the Land Transport
Authority, Radin Mas Community Arts and Cultural Club and professional
artists Mr Fish Jaafar.&nbsp; This initiative creates artwork which will
be displayed along the overhead bridge connecting Radin Mas Primary School
and CHIJ St Theresa’s Convent.&nbsp; It will feature a collage of various
scenes, objects and people that represent our vibrant Radin Mas Primary
School and community, whilst enhancing the aesthetic appeal of the overhead
bridge.&nbsp; Students, Staff, Parents, Alumni, School Advisory Committee
and our Radin Mas Community are all on board!&nbsp; Watch this space!</p>
