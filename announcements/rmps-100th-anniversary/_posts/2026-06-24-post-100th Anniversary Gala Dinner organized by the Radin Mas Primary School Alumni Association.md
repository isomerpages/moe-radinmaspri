<p>As part of Radin Mas Primary School’s 100th Anniversary celebrations,
the Radin Mas Primary School &nbsp;Alumni Association will be hosting a
special Alumni Dinner in the evening of Sunday, 22 November 2026.</p>
<p>Join us as we gather across generations to celebrate 100 years of memories,
friendships, and shared experiences that shaped who we are today. Reconnect
with old classmates, relive treasured moments and create new memories together
as we celebrate this once-in-a-century milestone.</p>
<p>📅 Date: 22 November 2026, Sunday</p>
<p>🕖 Time: 6pm to 10pm</p>
<p>🏫 Venue: Radin Mas Primary School ( Level 2, Hall)</p>
<p>🎟️ Ticket Price: $100 per pax</p>
<p>Gather your old classmates and form your tables!&nbsp; We look forward
to welcoming you back home to celebrate this special occasion together.</p>
<p>For more information, please visit <a href="http://www.rmps-alumni.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.rmps-alumni.sg</a>
