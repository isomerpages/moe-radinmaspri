<p><strong>Centenary Anniversary Logo</strong>
<br>As our school marks its 100th anniversary, we are proud to unveil our
Centenary Anniversary Logo, celebrating a century of commitment to nurturing
generations of students and contributing to the community.</p>
<p><strong>Design Concept: A Century of Hearts Woven by People, Passion and Purpose</strong>
<br>Our 100th anniversary logo celebrates the People, Passion and Purpose
which define our school community.
<br>
<br>Each dot represents one of our People - students, educators and stakeholders,
united by Care, Resilience and Excellence over the century.&nbsp; The interconnecting
lines represent our bonds while the vibrant red embodies our Passion in
education.</p>
<p>Framed by our historic and current school buildings, the design bridges
Our Heritage with Our Future, honouring where we began and underscoring
our enduring Purpose to mould the Future of our Nation.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Thank you to every student, educator and stakeholder who has been part
of this incredible journey.</p>
<p>Here’s to honouring the past and inspiring the future.</p>
